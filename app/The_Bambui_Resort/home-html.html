Welcome to The Bambui Resort, a place where you will have the opportunity to relax, learn and have a bit of taste of our culture, have some fun taking a ride in the park. You will also have the oportunity to visit and see nature as it was created, the untuoched natural resources we preserve, walk along the plains of Alegefor which is the home of our friendly red nose monkeys. You will also want to visit the home of the Turacos and our gorilla reserve, and have a taste our natural spring waters tapped underneath from the source at Munkuo.

The location of the kingdom in the heart of the town, and capital of Tubah Local Government Area with it plain nature also give a plus mark to the park. This makes movement very easy at all seasons.
